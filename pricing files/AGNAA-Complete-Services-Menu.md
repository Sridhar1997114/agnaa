## 📌 HOW THIS MENU WORKS

**Pick services. We quote exactly.**

Every service has:
- **Base Price** — Starting point
- **Per Sft Rate** — Scales with project size
- **Min/Max Range** — Floor & ceiling
- **Complexity Factor** — Adjusts for difficulty
- **Delivery** — How long it takes
- **Revisions** — How many changes included

### Key Principle: Two Types of Per Sft Pricing

| Pricing Type | Used For | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Per Sft (Plan)** | Floor plans, working drawings, MEP layouts | 1,000 sft built-up area = 1,000 sft plan |
| **Per Sft (Elevation)** | Elevation drawings, 3D renders, exterior models | 40 ft high × 60 ft wide facade = 2,400 sft elevation |
