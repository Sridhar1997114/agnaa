## EXECUTION STRICT RULES (Non-Negotiable Protocol)

### E1: Execution Starts Only After Design is Signed Off
No contractor begins work without client-signed drawing approval.
Any execution done on verbal approval = client's risk entirely. Documented in agreement.

### E2: Agnaa is Design Architect, Not Contractor
Agnaa manages design intent, not labour supervision daily.
Site visits are scheduled, not on-call.
Emergency site visit = 2× standard visit rate.

### E3: Variation Order First, Work After
Any site change — no matter how small — requires a Variation Order signed before execution.
"We'll adjust later" is not a protocol at Agnaa. There is no "later."

### E4: Contractor Payment Verification
Agnaa verifies contractor bills before client pays.
This is included in the 12–15% project management fee.
Unverified bills are not Agnaa's responsibility.

### E5: Completion Certificate
Issued by Agnaa only after:
- All drawings are executed as designed
- All pending variations are settled
- Final payment to Agnaa is received
The Completion Certificate is Agnaa's quality endorsement — it is never issued under pressure.
